Your Privacy, Clearly Explained

AJH's Vault is made for fun — not for collecting your personal information. We do not personally store or share any data about you. However, this site does use Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which may collect anonymous usage data or display relevant ads.

What We Don't Personally Collect

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No account creation
  • No browsing history stored by us
  • No IP tracking by AJH's Vault

What We Do Use

LocalStorage is used to save your game progress and preferences on your own device. This is never sent to our servers or shared with anyone.

  • Your progress is saved locally using localStorage
  • We can't see it, access it, or track it
  • You can clear it anytime by deleting site data in your browser settings
